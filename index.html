Click on any of the pathways below to learn more about it!

What is CTE?

Career and technical education (CTE) provide technical education, training, and hands-on skills for a specific career pathway. CTE creates an opportunity for the students to save money while giving them a pathway to a higher paying career. The skills learned in CTE programs open doors for students to continue their education or build a career right out of high school.

What are the benefits?

Career pathways are a FREE way to learn and develop new skills that can be used in higher education or can be applied on the job. CTE pathways create new opportunities to get your foot in the door for a career or industry you are interested.
